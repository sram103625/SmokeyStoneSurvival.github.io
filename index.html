8 week program of outdoor survival classes for kids ages 7-16 in the greater Milwaukee area. Teaching practical and useful outdoor skills. Hosted every Friday from 5pm - 7pm.

Essential Skills

Every week covers a new hands on survival lesson

🔥

Fire Craft

Safe fire creation, sourcing natural fuel, and mastering 1 match challenge.

💧

Sourcing Water

Methods for finding and sourcing water, filtering and purifying water, and importance of water.

🍓

Foraging

Finding and identifying different wild fruits, greens and nuts.

🧭

Navigation and Safety

Different navigation techniques, how to signal help, and staying safe during adventures.
